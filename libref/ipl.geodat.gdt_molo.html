################ Standard Molodensky Datum Transformation ################## See NIMA TR 8350.2

WARNING NIMA TR 8350.2 contains Molodensky parameters to convert from an arbitrary datum to WGS84. To convert from datum A to datum B, I call molodensky(Ax-Bx,Ay-By,Az-Bz,Aa,Af,Ba,Bf) where Ax,Ay,Az are the shift to convert A to WGS84; Bx,By,Bz are the shift to convert B to WGS84; Aa,Af,Ba,Bf are the ellipsoid parameters.
