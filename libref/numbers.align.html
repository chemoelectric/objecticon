align(x, y)

Package :
numbers
Type :
procedure

Take two arbitrary values and returns two equivalent values which are of the same class. An Align record is returned.

First need is used to convert x and y from simple types (integer and real) and strings into a Dec, Rat or Cpx. Then, if the x and y aren't of the same resulting type, one of them is converted so their types are the same. Any conversion made will be exact.

Parameters :
x - something convertible to Dec, Rat or Cpx
y - something convertible to Dec, Rat or Cpx
