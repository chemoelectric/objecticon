public static

Parse a string s as a generic header consisting of key-values. The keys are comprised of TOKEN_CHARs, as are the values, unless enclosed in double quotes. Examples of s :- 
     a=100 b=200; c="hello world"
     x="backslash escapes \" are allowed"
