prime_factors(i)

Package :
ipl.factors
Type :
procedure

Generate the prime factors of positive integer i. A sequence of prime_factor records is generated, each giving a prime and an exponent. For example, prime_factors applied to 

    5913648 = (2^4) * (3^7) * (13^2)
generates :- 
   prime_factor(p=2;e=4)
   prime_factor(p=3;e=7)
   prime_factor(p=13;e=2)
