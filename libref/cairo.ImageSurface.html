new(a[])

public

Create a new instance. If one parameter is given, then that may be a Pixels instance, in which case it must have the format ImageSurface.FORMAT, and the new ImageSurface wraps the Pixels instance, and shares its data. Otherwise, the parameter may be raw image data, or a filename of an image, and a new ImageSurface with initialized content is created.

If two parameters are given, then they specify a width and height, and a new blank ImageSurface is created.
