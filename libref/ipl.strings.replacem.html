replacem(s, pairs[])

Package :
ipl.strings
Type :
procedure

Performs multiple replacements in the style of of replace(), where multiple argument pairs may be given, as in 

    replacem(s, "a", "bc", "d", "cd")
which replaced all "a"s by "bc"s and all "d"s by "cd"s. Replacements are performed one after another, not in parallel.
