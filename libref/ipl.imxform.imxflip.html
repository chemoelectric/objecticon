imxflip(rows, dir)

Package :
ipl.imxform
Type :
procedure

Flip image matrix. The possible values of dir are "h" (horizontal flip), "v" (vertical flip), "l" (left diagonal), and "r" (right diagonal). (The left diagonal extends from the upper left corner to the bottom right corner; the right diagonal from the upper right to the lower left.
