gedfind(g, s) -- find records by name from gedcom record

g is a gedcom record; s is a string of whitespace-separated words. gedfind() generates each INDI node for which every word of s is matched by either a word of the individual's name or by the birth year. Matching is case-insensitive. find individual by name
