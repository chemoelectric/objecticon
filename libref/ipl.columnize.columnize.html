columnize(l, screen_width, gutter, rowwise)

Package :
ipl.columnize
Type :
procedure

Columnize a list of items given their widths. A ColumnizeResult record is returned, giving the dimensions of the output as well as a list of the column widths.

Parameters :
l - a list of item widths
screen_width - the total width available
gutter - the desired space between columns
rowwise - a flag indicating whether output will be left to right (flag set), or top to bottom (flag unset).
